Step 3

Create a glyph filter by clicking the Glyph button on the toolbar.

Step 4

Click Accept to generate glyph with default values: Glyphs are three-dimensional arrows (determined by "Glyph Source"), they are oriented with the momentum vector (determined by the Orient check button and "Point Vectors" menu) and scaled with the density function (determined by "Scale Mode"). Please note that this will generate 56x32x24 glyphs which, depending on your processor and graphics card's performance might take some time. If you have a slow processor or graphics card, you might want to skip step 4 and move on to step 7 (if you have already created the glyph filter continue with step 5).
