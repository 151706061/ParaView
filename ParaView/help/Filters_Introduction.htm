Filters Introduction

Once you have loaded your data into ParaView, running filters on the data is very straightforward.  There are four filters in the toolbar – isosurface (contour), calculator, threshold, and glyph.  For any of these filters, you must click the “Accept” button (after you have chosen values for all the parameters) to cause the filter to execute.  You can also get to filters not listed in the toolbar from the VTK | Filters menu.
