Isosurface (Contour)

There is a button on the toolbar (labeled “Contour”) that allows you to create an isosurface.  After you have clicked the Contour button, you can choose which array to use for your contour from the PointScalars menu button.  You can select whether to compute normals, gradients, and scalars by clicking the appropriate checkboxes.  To enter a contour value, type it into the entry box labeled “New Value”, and click the “Add Value” button.  To remove a contour value from the list, click the value you wish to remove and click the “Delete Value” button.
